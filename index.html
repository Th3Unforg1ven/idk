Glitch Angel 6 · Void Diver 7 · Gloom Reaper 7 · Soul Alchemist 6 · Flesh Oracle 10

SIGNAL
Tracks Cortex-Net pressure. Each failed test adds +1 Signal. At Signal 5: SYNC EVENT — T-Corp controls the character for one Shot. Signal resets to 0.
OVERDRIVE
Group pool 0–5. Generated by critical (nat 12). Resets at end of session.
1 OD — Reroll any die
2 OD — Fixed max damage on next attack or offensive ability
3 OD — Automatic success without rolling, once per Scene
DAMAGE CLOCK
Each character has 12 segments. Mark segments equal to die result. Full clock = out of combat.
Nanobots / Recode Heal — die result in segments
Stim (item) — 4 segments
Rest between scenes — 3 segments
Rest between sessions — full reset
BEATS (2 per Shot/Turn)
Movement — move or reposition
Attack — 2d6 + attribute vs THR · DMG 1d6
Technical — use an ability
Interaction — use or interact with an item
Help — spend your Technical Beat so an ally rolls using your attribute instead of their own.
COUNTERSHOT (Reaction)
Used outside your Shot. Costs 1 Beat on your next Shot. Countershot abilities: Intercept, Redirect, Protect.
